A Legacy of Excellence

Four decades of transformative leadership in medical education and healthcare

A 40-Year Journey of Excellence

Prof. Dr. Muhammad Umar stands as a pioneering figure in Pakistan's medical education landscape. As the inaugural Rawalian Principal of Rawalpindi Medical College and the founding Vice Chancellor of Rawalpindi Medical University, he has dedicated his career to advancing medical education and healthcare delivery.

"In a testament to dedication and national commitment, Prof. Umar has served as Vice Chancellor since 2017 without accepting any pay, allowances, privileges, or official car from the Government of Punjab and HEC - a service entirely voluntary and exemplifying true leadership."

His remarkable journey began as a distinguished graduate of the second batch of Rawalpindi Medical College in 1981, where he graduated with distinction and received multiple academic awards. Throughout his career, Dr. Umar achieved numerous milestones, becoming the first Rawalian FCPS, Assistant Professor, Professor, and ultimately Principal at RMC.

Under his visionary leadership, Rawalpindi Medical University achieved remarkable recognition, being ranked as the top Medical University in Pakistan by Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Rankings in 2021 and 2023. The undergraduate MBBS program rose from 6th to 1st position in merit rankings within just three years.
